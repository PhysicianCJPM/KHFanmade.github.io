Characters

A boy chosen by the keyblade, a mysterious weapon.

Not long ago, Sora journeyed with Donald and Goofy in search of his longtime friend Kairi, who had been spirited away into the darkness.

In the end, they saved the world of all worlds from the darkness that was closing in.


Sora is the main protagonist and main playable character in Kingdom Hearts, Kingdom Hearts: Chain of Memories, Kingdom Hearts II, Kingdom Hearts 3D: Dream Drop Distance, and Kingdom Hearts III.

Sora is 16 yrs. old.



Sora's longtime friend. He had clad himself in darkness in order to wield it, and had even changed in appearance.

But when the real Ansem's Kingdom Hearts Encoder exploded, Riku returned to his own form.

You could say it was a miracle- worked by a heart no darkness could eclipse.

Riku was 5-years-old during Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep, 15-years-old at the beginning of Kingdom Hearts and Kingdom Hearts: Chain of Memories, and 16-years-old during the events of Kingdom Hearts II and Kingdom Hearts 3D: Dream Drop Distance.

Riku is also Sora's Rival



Long time friend of Riku and Sora, and one of the Seven Princesses with ties to the Keyholes.

She had lost her memories of Sora, after they returned, she wanted to see him more and more with every passing day.

Falling through an unexpected hole in the darkness, she arrived in Twilight Town and befriended Hayner, Pence, and Olette before being captured by Axel.

Kairi is fervently hoping to meet up with Sora once more.

Kairi was 4-years-old during Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep, 14-years-old at the start of Kingdom Hearts, and 15-years-old during the events of Kingdom Hearts II and Kingdom Hearts 3D: Dream Drop Distance.



The king of Disney Castle. He was the first to realize what a threat the darkness was, and the first to act.

King Mickey travels across the worlds, battling the Heartless and searching for an answer to the riddle of Organization XIII and the Nobodies.

After many great battles, the King seems even more determined than ever to fight against the darkness.

Though he is known as the King of Disney Castle, his true name is Mickey Mouse.

He is also a Keyblade Master.



Goofy is a major supporting protagonist that appears in the Kingdom Hearts series. Goofy is Captain of the Royal Knights of King Mickey's court. He and Disney Castle's court magician Donald Duck went out to search for the King, and ends up teaming with Sora for the rest of their journey.

Despite his position at Disney Castle, Goofy dislikes using weapons, instead using a simple shield in combat, and attempts to avoid fighting whenever possible, preferring to find peaceful solutions to problems. Though simple-minded and clumsy as always, and constantly the butt of comic relief, Goofy is the constant voice of optimism and, surprisingly, selectively perceptive, often noticing things others miss and keeping his cool when Sora and Donald lose it. True to his depiction in other Disney media, Goofy is a steadfast and reliable friend.



Donald Duck is a character from the Kingdom Hearts series. In Kingdom Hearts, Donald is the Royal Magician of Disney Castle and the royal assistant for King Mickey. As such, his weapon of choice is a Staff. He often provides comic relief more than advancing the plot despite the amount of camera time he has in the series. He is rash and has an aggressive personality, but he is loyal to his friends.

Though he initially only cares about Sora as a means to track down King Mickey, Donald soon grows to deeply care for him as a friend.


Story

Sora, Kairi, and Riku lives on Destiny Island; a world specifically for their own. All three of them wanted to explore other worlds and leave their home. Soon... what they wish for came true. Darkness engulfed Destinity Island and awoke Sora from his slumber. Sora ran straight the island. Afterwards, Sora sees riku went inside the darkness and Kairi was taken by it. Sora fought a gigantuar Heartless and ate the Destinty Island.




Sora was sent to another world called Traverse Town where all those whose worlds were eaten by the darkness goes. That's where Sora met Pluto, Leon, Aerith, Yuffie, and Cid. That is where Sora's first adventure began. Not soon after, Sora met Donald and Goofy whom were looking for their king; Mickey. Mickey told them to find the 'keybearer', which is Sora. They ventured worlds, there was Alice from Wonderland, Hercules, Alladin, Jack Skellington, Peter Pan, and Many more! They encountered creatures and friends from low and high places. And at last, they came a close to the end of their adventure when they saw Riku, Sora' bestfriend possessed by 'Ansem', a researcher of hearts. They beat ansem and saved kairi along with the world of all worlds from the darkness 'Ansem' was releasing from Kingdom Hearts. However, it is without no consequences; the sacrifice of Riku being trapped in Kingdom Hearts along with King Mickey.

After the adventure the three vowed to stay together, still, to find their King and bestfriend. That's when they were being followed by a black-hooded figure and took them to castle oblivion where they met Namine. A 'special' Nobody whom was created when Kairi lost her heart. After the events in Castle Oblivion, Namine's tampering with her power with memories, Sora began to forget who he was and who his friends were. In order to preserve his memories DiZ or better known as Ansem the wise, the true Ansem, developed a simulation to put Sora under suspended animation, along with Donald and Goofy to help recover his memories with the help of his Nobody Roxas.

That's where Ansem the wise, kidnapped Roxas and brought him to a simulated Twilight Town until Sora recovers and for Roxas to give his own memories so that Sora would awake again. Roxas broke out of the simulation and finally met his creator, Sora, stating "I guess my summer vacation is over", indicating that it's time for Sora to be awoken again for a new adventure. Sora, Donald, and Goofy all woke up in a laboratory clueless as to why there were there in the first place. A new adventure sets in...

The adventure gave Sora new clothes that has magical powers and a new determination to find his bestfriend. With Goofy and Donald besides him so that they could find their king. Sora once again ventured to new worlds, this time it included, Jack sparrow from the Pirates of the carribean, Simba, Mulan, and others. They all helped Sora opened doors to other worlds to find Riku and King mickey. After visiting many worlds the true culprit for another mayhem were Nobodies. Once a person becomes a heartless they leave behind a body without a soul and emotions. The higher nobodies are called Organization XIII.

The leader of the organization is none other than Ansem the wise's apprentice Xemnas. Xemnas is a Nobody of Xehanort whom Sora fought when Xehanort's Heartless possessed Riku on their last adventure. The fight occured in the Land that Never Was. Organization XIII castle. There Sora finally found Riku along with Kairi when she got kidnapped by Saix, Organization XIII no. VII. Together, Sora and Riku fought Xemnas through a familiar 'door' where Sora and Riku were on opposite sides. After a heated fight, they beat Xemnas and Sora and Riku are finally together again. However, they were trapped in the realm of Darkness. That's when they found a message from a bottle, and a ray of light shone to the two heroes. They returned to their home, Destiny Island. Finally, Kairi, Sora, and Riku are back to their home finally. Then they receive news from King Mickey...
